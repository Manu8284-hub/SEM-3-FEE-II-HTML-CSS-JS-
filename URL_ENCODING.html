URL ENCODING

Originl URL

https://example.com/search?query=helloWorld

Encoded URL

https://example.com/search?query=hello%20world

Some Common Encoded Characters:

  • Space --> %20
  • & --> %26
  • = --> %3D
  • ? --> %3F
